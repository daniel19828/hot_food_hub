We Are A Non Profit Orginization

About Hot Food Hub.com Inc., we are a non profit organization that has joined the fight to “End World Hunger” by connecting less fortunate people, single parents, children, teenagers, homeless individuals, and the millions of Americans that have recently felt the devastating effects of Covid-19 with a FREE hot meal.

At Hot Food Hub.Com Inc., We stay in direct contact with several churches and a variety of non-profit organizations in the Greater Seattle area, spanding from the Canadian border down near the Oregon border that offer free hot meals to everyone.

We spend countless hours investigating all the different government agencies, food banks, hot meal sites and community out-reach services that appear to be practical and rewarding for those who need this service. We list only those agencies that offer help to all people for FREE with absolutely no strings attached.

The ability to utilize this resource even 3 days a week to subsidize a person's income can make the difference so much that these hot meals can be the glimmer of light and hope that these people hold dear while they pray and work through the difficulties of their struggles and Covid-19.

We focus on everyone from all walks of life to enjoy the services that our great country offers. This is why we have designed and developed a user-friendly website that works a lot like an app, (without the logging on and signing up) to help you find a location that will provide you with a free hot meal and with just a push of a button Google Maps will take you there.

Hot Food Hub.com Inc. is completely non-profit and currently we are in need of a company automobile and funds to cover business expenses such as IT and Web Development costs in order to continue to upgrade our website UI/UX interface and functionality of our digital platform. These updates are crucial if we want to be able to provide our users with a practical platform that can offer them a tangible method in obtaining a 100% free hot breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

We are currently testing new code that will make this site even more user friendly by using green and red lights that will be displayed when specific establishments are serving hot meals and red lights to show that the hot meals are over for the day.

Outsourcing contract work to Computer Engineers in order to achieve and update the interface of hotfoodhub.com will cost an estimated $20,000. All donations are gratefully accepted no matter how big or small the donation is, and we thank our donors greatly.
