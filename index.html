The #1 Place to Find free Hot Meals!

About Hot Food Hub.com Inc., we are a non profit organization that has joined the fight to “End World Hunger” by connecting less fortunate people, single parents, children, teenagers, homeless individuals, and the millions of Americans that have recently felt the devastating effects of Covid-19 with a FREE hot meal.

At Hot Food Hub.Com Inc., we stay in direct contact with several churches and a variety of non-profit organizations in the Greater Seattle area that offer 100% free hot meals to anyone thats hungry. As of February, 2022 we have 19 different organizations that offer a wide variety of free hot food 7 days a week.

We spend countless hours investigating the different government agencies, food banks, hot meal sites, churches and community out-reach services that appear to be practical and rewarding for those who depend on this service. We list only those agencies that offer help to all people for FREE with absolutely no strings attached.

You are not required to pay any amount of money to eat at our listed locations. There are no restrictions whatsoever. There are a number of great places to eat at in the Seattle area that do require a person to be of a certain age or gender, we do not list those locations on hotfoodhub.com.

You can be sure that when you show up to one of the locations listed on our website you will enjoy a 100% FREE hot meal with nothing at all asked of you.

We encourage anyone that is going through a difficult time to utilize the service we offer and save money by eating at these 100% FREE hot meal locations. All of the organizations that we list on our web site are currently serving free hot meals at the said times, and all people are welcome with absolutely no exceptions.
